Building your map:

Customise and download free vector maps in GeoJSON format for your web apps and visualisations.

These maps are sourced from Natural Earth and are in the public domain, so you are free to use them in any way without attribution. Natural Earth shows de facto boundaries by default according to who controls the territory, versus de jure. Optional point-of-view (POV) variants are available for several dozen countries on their website.
